About Us

Established in 2022 in Patiala, India, Prime Exports & Trading (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted name in the global export industry, committed to delivering quality healthcare products to international markets. As a dynamic merchant exporter, we specialize in sourcing, stocking, and supplying a wide range of healthcare and wellness solutions with efficiency, reliability, and competitive pricing.

With a strong network of certified manufacturers and suppliers across India, we offer nutraceuticals, ayurvedic products, herbal solutions, OTC products, medical essentials, and wellness care products tailored to global demand.

Our capability to maintain ready stock availability ensures faster order processing and timely dispatch for urgent and bulk requirements.

We proudly deal in our own wellness brand "Vrikshayu Naturals", along with reputed third-party brands and generic products, giving our clients access to a diverse and competitive portfolio under one roof.

Driven by trust, quality, and customer satisfaction, we aim to build long-term partnerships worldwide by providing seamless export solutions, professional service, and dependable supply chains.
